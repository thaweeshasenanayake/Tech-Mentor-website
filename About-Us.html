ABOUT US

Tech Mentors is a pioneer higher educational institution in Sri Lanka and has proved 25 years of academic excellence. The Ministry of Higher Education (MOHE) has approved the recognition of these courses in the industry. Tech Mentors is an Internationally connect institute. Courses offered by Tech Mentors have been approved by the MOHE, UGC and SLASSCOM in Sri Lanka.

OUR VISION
Our Vision is Provide best skillful IT professionals to the industry.
Our mission is together with internationally reputed Educational Institutes and Industry best companies, to provide high quality educational programs, which will enhance the quality of the skills available to the job markets at an affordable level, thereby contributing to the development of economy.
